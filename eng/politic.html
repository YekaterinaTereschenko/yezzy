      The Company reserves the right to change and supplement the existing provisions of the Privacy Policy, and such changes and supplements will take precedence over the existing provisions of the Policy. It should be noted that the services provided may contain links to third-party websites that have their own privacy policies. In such cases, the Company is not responsible for the content of the third party's privacy policy or for compliance with its provisions. By providing their personal data, the Client fully agrees to the processing of their personal data in accordance with the provisions of the current Privacy Policy of the Company.

      Procedure for collecting, storing, and disposing of personal data

      The Company collects personal data only with the consent of the customer and only for the purpose of providing the customer with a service or service that requires the provision of personal data: purchase in an online store, participation in promotions, warranty service and maintenance. Various information security measures are applied (both technical and organizational) to prevent leakage of customer personal data.

      The maximum storage period for customer personal data is 75 years from the date of receipt by the company of private information. Otherwise, the company will store the customer's personal data until the end of its activity.

      Personal data or its parts must be destroyed, anonymized, or blocked to prevent further processing of this information.

      The Company collects personal data of the following categories of customers:

      Technical information:

      access time and IP address;

      Sources of access to the Company's Internet resources;

      Websites visited by the user;

      types of advertising panels;

      other technical information provided by the user's browser;

      Customer's phone number (when calling the numbers indicated on the Company's website).

      During registration on the site, the Company collects the customer's personal data for user authentication / service personalization / order delivery / customer feedback:

      User activity information:

      Information about agreements/contracts between the customer and the company;

      Information about inquiries and orders addressed to the company;

      Purposes of processing customer personal data:

      Sell products and provide other services to customers;

      Take into account the wishes of customers (when developing a new product or service);

      Inform the customer about promotions, discounts, and special offers by email and SMS;

      To provide customers with feedback.

      Do not transfer customer personal data to third parties, except in the following cases:

      obtain explicit consent from the user to provide his personal data to a third party;

      at the request of law enforcement agencies in accordance with applicable law;

      merger or acquisition by the company.

      Use of cookies and tabs

      Cookies allow us to collect the following information about the customer: IP address / operating system / type browser / URL of the referring website. This is necessary to improve the services that the company provides to the customer (remembering the password on the site when closing the browser, traffic analysis, etc.). If you wish, you can at any time prevent tracking your activity by disabling cookies.

      User rights regarding personal data

      All customers can edit their personal data in the User's Cabinet. If the user expresses a desire to stop processing their personal data, they must contact the company.
